## 2025-05-20 - Adventure Dark Glassmorphism Redesign
**Learning:** Implementing a cohesive dark theme with glassmorphism requires a centralized token system (globalStyles.js) to ensure consistent backdrop-filters and border-opacities across diverse views like Chat, Profile, and Dashboards.
**Action:** Always start by defining core glassmorphic surface tokens (bgBlur, borderAlpha) before styling individual components to maintain visual unity in complex interfaces.
